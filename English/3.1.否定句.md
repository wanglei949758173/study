# 否定句
英文中否定句通过**not**来体现,**not** 需要放在**助动词**之后

助动词：例如be

## 例子
### is
```
This is my umbrella.
This isn't my umbrella.

This is my suit.
Is this your suit?
This isn't my suit.
```

### are
```
They are my friends.
Are they your friends?
They aren't my friends.
```

### am
```
I am a teacher.
Are you a teacher?
I'm not a teacher.
```
