# 特殊疑问句综合练习
用**what,who,how,whose**对加粗部分进行提问：

1. This is `a dog`.
  `What is this?`
2. That is `a car`.
  `What is that?`
3. My hair is `brown`.
  `What color is your hair?`
4. Elaine is `our teacher`.
  `Who is Elaine?`
5. That is `Mike's` car.
  `Whose car is that?`
6. Helen is `well`.
  `How is helen?`
